Authentication

Verify your identity

Instructions for authentication:
  1. Download the test data file
  2. Convert hex to binary base64 --decode message.base64 > message.bin
    Converts base64 representation back to binary
  3. Sign with your private key openssl dgst -sha256 -sign private.key -out signature.bin message.bin
    Creates digital signature using your private RSA key
Important: The signature.bin file is what you need to upload below. Never share your private.key file!
